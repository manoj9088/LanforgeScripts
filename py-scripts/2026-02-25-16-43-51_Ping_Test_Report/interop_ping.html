Objective

The objective of the ping test is to evaluate network connectivity and measure the round-trip time taken for data packets to travel from the source to the destination and back. It helps assess the reliability and latency of the network, identifying any packet loss, delays, or variations in response times. The test aims to ensure that devices can communicate effectively over the network and pinpoint potential issues affecting connectivity.
